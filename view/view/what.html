Welcome to our website, where you can enjoy reading and sharing jokes. We provide a platform for users to express their creativity and humor through joke writing.

To show appreciation for a joke and give credit to the author, simply click on the "W" button located on the right side of the joke. By doing so, you can save the joke and view your saved jokes later by clicking on the "Your W" button. Your saved jokes are cleared every 10 days, so be sure to save them.

Please note that users are allowed to publish one joke per hour to maintain the quality and variety of content on our site.

We constantly keep track of the number of "W" clicks on each joke, which allows us to showcase the top performers of the day. You can see the nicknames of the daily leaders on our website.

Furthermore, we have an exciting competition to determine the best jokester of the month. The winners will receive a congratulatory email sent directly to their registered email address. You can also check your own results on the leaderboard page, located below the top three positions.

Join our community of joke enthusiasts and let your creativity shine. Start sharing your jokes and enjoy the laughter!
